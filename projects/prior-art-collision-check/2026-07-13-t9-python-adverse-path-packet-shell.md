## Fill Discipline

A later filled packet should separate four blocks:

1. Direct source facts from the selected PR, issue, recognition signal, or
   sponsorship source.
2. Reviewer inferences about record, rationale, recognition, reward, authority,
   and future eligibility.
3. Local comparison pressure on contribution trace, loss notes, retained record,
   retained value, maintainer authority, or governance transition.
4. Protected non-conclusions that keep T9 unresolved until governed review.

If any block cannot be kept separate, the packet should remain incomplete rather
than becoming a weak verdict.
